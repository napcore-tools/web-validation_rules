# Notes

SERVICE JOURNEY INTERCHANGEs take place at a CONNECTION. Stops linked by a CONNECTION should not be further apart than found in a large interchange - e.g. 500m for rail 2km for air. 100m for bus. coach. Upstream numbering clash: T.21.05 is assigned to 2 distinct rules in the source sheet; local -b/-c suffixes keep records apart.
