# Notes

A GROU OF LINEs can distinguish one specific LINE as being the primary LINE for the group. This LINE should also be declared as a member of the group Upstream numbering clash: T.02.11 is assigned to 2 distinct rules in the source sheet; local -b/-c suffixes keep records apart.
